Rice

Description

Quick and easy Seasoned Rice dish bursting with amazing flavors from herbs and spices. A buttery, herby, and savory side dish that can be eaten on its own and goes pretty well with anything, too.

    Ingredients
    butter
    uncooked long grain white rice
    garlic and onion powder
    salt
    oregano, thyme and parsley
    paprika
    pepper flakes
    bouillion
    Instructions
  1. heat butter in a pot
  2. heat until it simmers
  3. wash several cups of rice twice
  4. transfer rice to pot
  5. add two cups of water per cup of uncooked rice
  6. add all the above mentioned spices into the pot
  7. stir the rice and spices throughly
  8. leave on the stove for 20-30
  9. Cool and serve
