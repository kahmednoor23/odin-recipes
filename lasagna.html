Lasagna

Description

This is a classic recipe which is made easy with a meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly. This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

    Ingredients
    1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles
    olive oil
    ground beef
    one medium onion
    bell pepper
    garlic
    crushed tomatoes
    tomato paste
    garlic powder
    1/4 cup sugar
    salt
    Instructions
  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic, add back the beef
  4. Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Heat the oven to 375 degrees Farenheight
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
  9. Cool and serve
